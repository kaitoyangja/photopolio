About Shepherd

Seoul-born. Sound-driven. Strategically minded.

Shepherd is a Seoul-based musician and data strategy student who weaves together the realms of music and analytics, creating a seamless flow between intuition and logic.

While more comfortable with Logic Pro than Python, Shepherd embraces the hybrid nature of being both an artist and an analyst. Currently researching alternative and psychedelic soundscapes, Shepherd explores the boundaries between experimental hip-hop, ambient textures, and mind-expanding sonic experiences.

The sound research focuses on creating immersive audio environments that challenge conventional listening patterns, blending organic instrumentation with digital manipulation to craft unique sonic narratives that transport listeners to otherworldly dimensions.
